Start Again?

"The cake is a lie" is a reference to a game called Portal. Throughout Portal, the player is required to solve puzzles and is promised a cake in the end for their trouble. However, it becomes slowly more evident that the robot voice that is telling you to complete these tasks is actually attempting to murder the player. There is no cake in the game, just death hence the joke 'the cake is a lie'. Portal starts off as a simple puzzle game where the player has to figure out how to escape each room by creating portals. As levels progress, they get harder and start to include ways the player can die. At the end, the player must fight the machine in order to survive. Portal starts off as a simple puzzle game but then becomes a violent survival based game. The game deceives you into thinking it is just a normal problem solving game without much of a story. As the game goes on, more and more is revealed and the machines words and actions get increasingly hostile.
