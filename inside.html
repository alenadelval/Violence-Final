The creatures from Outlast will violently kill you if you are caught. There is no form of self defense as the game mechanics are based on stealth. Disturbing images and violent actions are depicted through out the game. From torture to gore, Outlast warns users of its mature content. It uses these elements to tell an overall highly rated story and game. Violence is the key feature in this horror game like most others.
